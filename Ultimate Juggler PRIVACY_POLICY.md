# Privacy Policy for Ultimate Juggler

**Last Updated: December 15, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Ultimate Juggler ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile game. We are committed to being fully transparent about our data practices.

## Summary (TL;DR)

- **We do NOT collect any personal information directly**
- **We do NOT have user accounts or logins**
- **We do NOT store any data on our servers** (we don't have servers)
- **Google AdMob displays ads** and may collect data for personalized advertising
- **Google Play Billing handles purchases** - we never see your payment information
- **Game progress is stored locally on your device only**

---

## Information We Collect

### Information We Collect Directly: **NONE**

Ultimate Juggler does not directly collect any personal information. We do not:
- Require account creation
- Ask for your name, email, or any personal details
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, photos, or files
- Record audio or video
- Collect device identifiers ourselves

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to us:
- High scores
- Game settings (sound preferences)
- Purchased themes and premium status
- Game progress

This data stays on your device. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.

---

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

### 1. Google AdMob (Advertising)

We display advertisements through Google AdMob to support free access to the game.

**What AdMob May Collect:**
- Advertising ID (can be reset in your device settings)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- IP address (for approximate location/country)
- App usage data
- Ad interaction data (clicks, views)

**How to Control Ad Data:**
- **Reset Advertising ID:** Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID
- **Opt-out of Personalized Ads:** Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **Purchase Premium Bundle:** Removes all ads from the game ($4.99)

**AdMob Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 2. Google Play Billing (In-App Purchases)

If you make a purchase (themes or premium bundle), the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play.

**What We Receive from Google:**
- Confirmation that a purchase was made
- Product ID (which item was purchased)
- Purchase token (to verify the purchase is legitimate)

**What We Do NOT Receive:**
- Your payment information (credit card, etc.)
- Your Google account email
- Your billing address
- Any financial details

**Google Play Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy
